Your Questions, Our Answers: Navigating Your Path in Australia. Find answers to common queries about visas, education, health insurance, and more in our comprehensive FAQs section.

Your Questions, Answered

We understand that embarking on your Australian journey comes with questions and uncertainties. That's why we've compiled this comprehensive list of frequently asked questions to provide clarity and guidance. If you can't find the answer you're looking for, don't hesitate to contact us. We're here to assist you every step of the way.

Visa FAQs

  • What Are the Different Types of Australian Visas?
    Australia offers a wide range of visas, including skilled, family, student, visitor, and working holiday visas. Each category has specific subclasses tailored to different purposes.
  • How Do I Know Which Visa Is Right for Me?
    We offer a visa assessment service to evaluate your eligibility and recommend the most suitable visa based on your qualifications and goals.
  • What Are the Key Eligibility Criteria for Australian Visas?
    Eligibility criteria vary by visa category but often include factors like age, skills, health, character, and English language proficiency.
  • What Documents Do I Need for My Visa Application?
    The required documents depend on the type of visa you're applying for. We provide a checklist and guidance on gathering the necessary documents.
  • How Long Does It Take to Process a Visa Application?
    Processing times vary by visa type and can range from a few weeks to several months. Due to the individual nature of each application, we can only provide you with a time estimate once one of our migration consultants has evaluated it. Get in touch with us right now to arrange a no-obligation consultation with one of our migration experts. We'll keep you informed throughout the process.
  • Can I Include My Family Members in My Visa Application?
    Many visa categories allow family members to be included in the application. We'll advise you on family inclusion options.
  • Am I eligible to work in Australia?
    Your visa type determines if you can work in Australia. Some visas do not allow work, some have work limits, and some allow full-time work. Check your visa grant letter for details. If you have a visa but are unsure about work or want to change it, contact us for help. We have expert migration agents who can advise and assist you.

Education Visa FAQs

  • How Do I Choose the Right Course and Institution in Australia?
    Our education consultants offer personalized guidance to help you select the best course and institution that align with your career goals.
  • What Are the English Language Requirements for Studying in Australia?
    English language proficiency requirements vary by institution and course. We'll assist you in meeting these requirements.
  • Are Scholarships Available for International Students?
    Yes, there are various scholarships and financial aid options available for international students. We'll help you explore these opportunities.
  • Can I Work While Studying in Australia?
    Yes, many student visas allow you to work part-time while studying. We'll provide information on your work rights.

General FAQs

  • What Sets Bhargo Education Apart from Other Consultancies?
    We offer personalized, client-centric services, backed by a team of registered migration agents and education consultants. Our commitment to your success is our top priority.
  • How Can I Contact Bhargo Education?
    You can reach us through our contact page, email, or phone. We are here to assist you and answer your questions.
  • Do You Provide Ongoing Support After Visa Grant?
    Yes, we offer ongoing support to help you settle in Australia. From finding accommodation to understanding your rights, we are here for you.
  • Is Bhargo Education Registered and Accredited?
    Yes, we are registered migration agents and accredited education consultants. We adhere to the highest ethical and professional standards.
  • How can Bhargo Education help me if I am already in Australia?

    Bhargo Education can help you with various visa and consultation services, whether you are onshore or offshore. We can help you with any of the following:

    • Extending your current visa validity
    • Applying for a new visa
    • Getting Australian Permanent Residency
    • Applying for Australian Citizenship
    • Communicating with the Department of Home Affairs
    • Cancelling your visa
    • Appealing against the Department of Home Affairs’ decision
