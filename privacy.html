Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10th Oct, 2023

Welcome to Bhargo Education. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data. By accessing or using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

We protect your personal information in following ways:

  • - We only gather information that is necessary to assist you with your request or to present you with the information you have requested in a manner that is tailored to your preferences.
  • - We shall not keep any of your personal information. If we no longer require it and you do not wish for it to be retained, we will remove it.
  • - We will make every effort to explain everything we do with your information because we want you to understand everything we do with it.
  • - There will be no surprises - you will be in complete control of what and how you get communications from us.

Information Collection and Use

We may collect several types of information for various purposes, including but not limited to:

  • - Personal Data: When you interact with our website, you may voluntarily provide us with personal information, such as your name, email address, and contact details. We use this information to communicate with you and provide relevant updates.
  • - Usage Data: We may collect data about your interactions with the Service, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and pages viewed. This information helps us analyze trends, administer the site, and improve user experience.

We may collect this information directly from you, or from the third-party sources such as social media platforms

Use of Your Information

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • - We use your information to provide services and information related to Australian visas, education, and migration.
  • - Your data may also be used to communicate with you, respond to inquiries, and improve our services.
  • - To comply with legal obligations

We may also use the provided information for other purposes with the users permission

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal information with third parties under certain circumstances, including:

  • - Service Providers: We may engage third-party companies and individuals to assist with the Service's operation and maintenance. These service providers may have access to your personal information to perform tasks on our behalf.
  • - Analytics partners who help us improve your user experience and measure the performance of our websites and tools
  • - Legal authorities as required by the law or to protect ours as well as users rights and property(s)
  • - We do not sell or rent the users information without their explicit consent.

We may also share information with other third parties with the users permission

Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page.
